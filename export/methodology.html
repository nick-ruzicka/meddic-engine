Methodology

Every Score Has a Reason.

The system behind the scores — how 20 target institutions become your outreach queue.
Step 1 · Discovery

From 7,115 ICP firms to 20 named accounts

We start with 7,115 ICP-qualified firms from SEC filings. The top 500 by AUM enter Tier 2 monitoring — firmographic scoring only, no contact enrichment. When a Tier 2 firm triggers a signal, it promotes to Tier 1 for full enrichment and contact scoring. The 20 Tier 1 firms are hand-curated top-tier institutions with named contacts, verified emails, and full account briefs.

Step 2 · Signal Collection

What we look for

Four categories of signal, each scored for recency and relevance. The system watches for evidence that a firm is actively transforming — not just capable of it.
Pain

AlphaSense Frustration

Pricing complaints, workflow friction, RAG failures on long documents — signals that the incumbent isn't doing the job.
We're paying AlphaSense a fortune and still can't get a clean answer across the full CIM. Spending half my time re-asking the same question.
Evaluation

Piloting & RFPs

Testing language, vendor comparisons, pilot programs. These firms are in-market now — the window is open.
Kicking off vendor eval for deal-team AI in Q2. Shortlist includes , AlphaSense, Rogo. Compliance review starts next week.
Transformation

AI-First Announcements

Firm-wide AI mandates, dedicated Head of AI hires, portfolio-wide deployment plans. The buying decision is already made.
Thrilled to announce our portfolio-wide AI rollout — building the deal team of the future with production infrastructure live this quarter.
Competitor

Displacement Openings

Dissatisfaction with AlphaSense, Bloomberg, or Rogo. Price, depth, or workflow — each is a distinct sales story.
Rogo is fine for quick Q&A but breaks on multi-doc synthesis. We need something that actually reads the full data room.
Step 3 · Scoring

How we score each contact

A weighted composite across four dimensions. Every dimension is scored 0-100 by Claude given the firm, contact, and signal context.
Score = 0.30·ICP + 0.25·AI + 0.25·Reach + 0.20·Fresh
30%
ICP Fit
Firm type, AUM tier, workflow fit. Tier 1 megafunds score highest.
25%
AI Readiness
Buying stage, AI hiring, published AI strategy. Evidence the firm is moving.
25%
Reachability
Verified email, named decision-maker, title match. No generic inboxes.
20%
Signal Freshness
Days since most recent signal. Last 30 days score strongly; older decays.
Step 4 · Skill Router

How the AI adapts per firm

Before scoring, a deterministic router selects which skill sections to load into Claude's prompt. A PE firm sees different scoring rules than an investment bank; a firm with known objections sees compliance-weighted language.
